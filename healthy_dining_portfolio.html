Healthy Dining App

Helping users make healthy and hygienic food choices while eating out

Role
UX/UI Designer
Timeline
6 weeks
Tools
Figma, Miro, Notion
Deliverables
Research, Wireframes, Prototype, Testing
01 — OVERVIEW

Project Overview

Eating out often means compromising on health and hygiene. This project aims to design an intuitive app that helps users find healthy, hygienic food choices fast.

🎯

Problem Statement

Eating outside often compromises health and hygiene. Users struggle to identify which dishes are truly healthy or safe.

💡

Project Goal

Design an intuitive app that helps users find healthy, hygienic food choices fast with clear information about nutrition and cleanliness.

👥

Target Users

Busy working professionals, college students, and anyone who eats out regularly but wants to maintain a healthy diet.

02 — RESEARCH & INSIGHTS

Understanding User Needs

We conducted user interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis to uncover habits and pain points

70%

Check nutrition info only if obvious

65%

Would use filters for dietary needs

80%

Agreed hygiene ratings boost confidence

👁

Visibility is Critical

Users want at-a-glance indicators (like badges or scores) for healthy choices rather than reading through detailed descriptions.

🔒

Trust & Transparency

Reliable data (nutrition facts, cleanliness ratings) builds confidence and helps users make informed decisions.

⚡

Simplicity Matters

Users prefer quick filters and recommendations over reading full menus, especially when making quick decisions.

05 — IDEATION & FEATURES

Feature Prioritization

Using MoSCoW method to prioritize features based on user needs and business goals

Must-Have

  • Health score for each dish
  • Basic filters (vegetarian, gluten-free)
  • Hygiene ratings for restaurants
  • Search functionality

Should-Have

  • Personalized meal recommendations
  • Ingredient breakdowns
  • Nutrition facts (calories, macros)
  • User reviews

Could-Have

  • Social sharing features
  • Gamification elements
  • Friends' recommendations
  • Rewards for healthy choices

Won't-Have (Yet)

  • In-app ordering
  • Restaurant reservations
  • Community forums
  • Meal planning calendar

09 — FINAL SOLUTION

The Complete Experience

A polished, user-friendly interface that achieves our design goals

1 Home Feed with Recommendations

Personalized suggestions for restaurants and dishes right on the home screen with health highlights like "Top 3 High Protein Dishes Today"

2 Health & Hygiene Scoring

Simple algorithm computes health scores based on calories and nutrition balance, integrated with official hygiene grade data

3 Personalized Dashboard

Users can set diet preferences and see stats like "You saved 500 calories this week!" with badges for achieving health milestones

10 — REFLECTIONS

Key Learnings

Important lessons that will inform future design decisions

🔐

Trust Through Transparency

Users trust the app when health scores and hygiene data are clearly visible. Providing transparent information builds confidence over generic claims.

⚖️

Balance Detail & Simplicity

There's a fine line between enough information and overload. Present critical data at a glance while allowing deeper details on demand.

🚀

Future Potential

Opportunities for gamifying healthy choices and community features. Staying iterative will be key to continue improving the experience.
